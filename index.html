Questions

Common Questions

Is this open to Christians and people of all faiths?▼

Absolutely. As these photographs show, Popes John Paul II, Benedict XVI, and Francis all placed prayer notes at the Western Wall. Christians, Jews, Muslims, and people of every faith are welcome. The Wall receives all who come with sincere hearts.

How do I know my prayer is actually placed at the Wall?▼

Every prayer is photographed at the Western Wall with a timestamp — showing your prayer tucked between the sacred stones. This photograph is emailed directly to you. Premium tiers include video footage of the placement.

Is my prayer kept completely private?▼

Yes — 100%. Your prayer goes directly to print without being read by anyone. We never store, share, or access the content of your words. What you write is between you and God.

How long does my prayer stay at the Wall?▼

Notes are collected twice yearly by the Western Wall Heritage Foundation and buried with reverence at the Mount of Olives. Your prayer becomes part of this sacred tradition permanently.

Can I pray for someone else?▼

Yes. Many people send prayers on behalf of sick parents, struggling children, or friends who need God's intervention. This is one of the most meaningful uses of the service — interceding for someone you love.
